How it works

Scroll through a feed of photos within a specific location or worldwide. Users are free to post photos within their locations feed or the worldwide feed and interact with each other on a worldwide scale through up votes, down votes, and comments. Essentially, every photo posted is entered into two competitions; a local competition and a worldwide competition. This gives users the opportunity to play for two prizes at once!

Here is how you win

Post pictures in categories unique to your location and view photos posted in categories from past competitions. The sum of your highest voted photo in each category is taken to product your “Current Votes.” The user with the highest “Current Votes” at the end of the competition period is granted the prize in the given location!

Users are given the opportunity to customize their profile page with a profile picture and background picture. The profile displays all of the users photos in the users preference of a grid or list view. Additionally, it displays the total number of photos posted, the “Current Votes” for that competition, and the “Total Votes” across all competitions.
