Oleg Novoselskyy's Story

Oleg Novoselskyy is a student at FSU in Tallahassee, Florida. He moved to the United States from Ukraine when he was 5 years old and is fluent in the Russian language. He is currently completing his last semester to earn his B.A. in criminology.

He also develops websites at Web Media Concepts L.L.C. and is the founder of NovoLyt and the NovoLyt Lab Projects. His interest in developing led him to explore many areas in the tech field. He works with websites, apps, and even work with robotics. He also designs graphics and can touch up photos/edit movies. Some of his interests include reading books, hanging out with friends, and investing.
